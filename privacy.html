Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At Self Reflection, we prioritize your privacy. This policy outlines the data we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to protect your personal information.

2. Data Collection

We collect personal data that you provide when creating an account, such as your email address and any content you add to your journal. Additional information may be collected through cookies to improve the user experience.

3. Data Use

Your data is used to enhance your experience on Self Reflection, including personalizing content and providing relevant security measures. We do not share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent.

4. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Despite these efforts, no method of data transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

5. User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us through the provided contact information.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page or sending an email notification.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at privacy@selfreflection.com.
