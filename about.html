About Self Reflection

Founded in July 2024 by Mahesh Sharma, Self Reflection serves as an online journal that provides a secluded and secure atmosphere where users can embrace introspection and self-care.

  • Capture your daily experiences with ease.
  • Delve into your emotions and reflect deeply.
  • Set goals and track progress in a judgment-free space.
  • Enjoy a minimalistic design while maintaining high security.
  • Write privately on our intuitive platform.
